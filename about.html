Our Story

We're on a mission to bring the beauty and health benefits of nature into
every home, one plant at a time.

Rooted in Passion

Plants Nursery started in 2015 as a small backyard project in Green
Valley. What began as a personal collection of rare monsteras quickly
grew into a community of plant lovers seeking high-quality,
sustainably grown greenery.

Today, we operate a state-of-the-art greenhouse and ship thousands
of plants across the country, but our core values remain the same:
quality, sustainability, and education.
